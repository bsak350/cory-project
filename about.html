Meet the CEO, Cory Martin

Cory Martin founded Martinesque, LLC in 2017 after 4 years as the Director of Brand Marketing and Management and 8 years as an Independent Brand Manager for Jolity, Inc. Her expertise comes from 3 years as a Premium Support Technical Account Manager and Seller Support Associate for Amazon. Cory has been able to take her extensive e-commerce background and create her own vision as a self-driven consultant. She has a passion for educating her retail partners on best practices to promote growth opportunities for their businesses on the Amazon platform (seller central and vendor central). Her excellent verbal and written communication skills form collaborative relationships with her brands and drive consumer traffic to targeted products, increasing brand exposure and sales.

Cory holds a Master's of Arts (MA) in Forensic Psychology from the University of North Dakota and Bachelor's of Science (BS) in Applied Studies in Psychology and Criminal Justice from the University of Minnesota-Crookston.
